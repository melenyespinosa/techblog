Blown to Bits

Meleny Espinosa

Knowing Where You Are is one of the sections I read from Blown to Bits. This section was about how Global Position Systems also known as GPS know exactly where you are at all times. GPS can be used in cellphones, cars, etc and can provide you with different types of information. For example if you are on a mountain, a GPS can inform you about how high up the mountain you are.

The Parking Garage Knows More Than You Think talks about parking garages and privacy. Every car that enters the airport garage has its license plate photographed at the same time as the ticket is being taken. If the tickets are accidentally swapped then one car will not be allowed to leave. This raises many questions like who else gets access to the license plate numbers? and how long is the data retained?

Why We Lost Our Privacy, or Gave It Away explains to us how we give away our private information without knowing. We lose control of our personal information because of things we do to ourselves, and the things that others do to us. An interesting point from the reading that was made is that we give away information about ourselves voluntarily.

This makes me alert because it makes me realize that our personal and private information can be seen my other people and we have no control over it even if we might think we do. These readings made me realize that we give out information about ourselves unintentionally without even noticing which scares me and makes me anxious. In my opinion, I believe that no one has privacy because of all these factors like GPS, our phones, our surroundings.
